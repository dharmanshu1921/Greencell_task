How to Use

Follow these simple steps to analyze your fleet's voltage data:

  1. Prepare a CSV file with columns: Timestamp (format: DD-MM-YYYY HH:MM:SS) and Values (voltage readings)
  2. Click on "Select CSV File" to choose your data file from your computer
  3. Verify that the correct file is selected - you'll see the filename below the button
  4. Click "Analyze Data" to upload and process your voltage readings
  5. View the comprehensive analysis with interactive visualizations and insights
